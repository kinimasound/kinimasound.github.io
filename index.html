About
Kinima Sound is a creative music and sound design studio based in London. We make bespoke music and sound for moving image, from motion graphics to branded content, installations to film.

Our approach is to find creative opportunity and inspiration in every project and through the unique challenges they present. We understand the importance of sound in moving image and our years of experience - in composition, sound generation and manipulation, studio recording and mixing - allow us to make unique, forward-thinking work with precision and ambition.

We believe in creating music and sound that is inspiring, vivid and distinct.
